Introduction

Welcome to Halloween Drop n Pop. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our game.

Information We Collect

When you use Halloween Drop n Pop, we may collect the following information:

  • Usage statistics and gameplay analytics

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the game experience

  • Develop new features and content

Third-Party Services

Our game may use third-party services for analytics and advertising, including:

  • Google AdMob for displaying advertisements

These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies.

Data Storage

Most of your game data is stored locally on your device. We do not store personal information on our servers unless explicitly stated.

Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising
  • Uninstall the game at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
